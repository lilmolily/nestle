About Us

Founded in 2019 by a small team of designers and garment technicians in Manila, Core Zip started with a clear goal: make dependable outerwear that feels like it was made for everyday life. We develop and prototype locally, then produce in small, carefully vetted runs with family-owned factories to keep quality high and waste low.

We focus on honest product design — clear measurements, durable materials, and construction that stands up to frequent wear. Our collections prioritize useful features (thoughtful pockets, reliable zippers, and weather-ready fabrics), inclusive sizing, and easy care. We also work to reduce our environmental impact by choosing recycled fibers where feasible, minimizing excess packaging, and partnering with nearby suppliers to shorten supply chains. When you shop with Core Zip, you're supporting a small team committed to responsible sourcing, practical design, and helpful customer service — including easy exchanges within 14 days and prompt replies from our support team.
